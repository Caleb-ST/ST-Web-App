02 — Warranty

Supplier Warranty Information

Search suppliers and view warranty processes.

A warranty covers faults caused by manufacturing defects only. It does not cover normal wear and tear, consumable parts (such as drill chucks, brushes, blades, or belts), accidental damage, misuse, or modifications. Any damage caused by improper use, lack of maintenance, or external factors is excluded. If the product shows signs of physical damage, tampering, or unauthorised repair, the warranty is void. Proof of purchase is required for all claims. All M & H class vacuums must be submitted directly through the supplier by the customer due to hazardous material concerns.
